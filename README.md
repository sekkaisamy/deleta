Deleta — Disk Analysis • Safe Cleanup • You stay in control
A smart disk-analysis assistant that helps you free up space safely.

Tired of hunting for files to delete while your disk space keeps running low? Deleta helps you understand what is really using your storage, so you can clean up confidently and consciously.

Deleta does not delete files automatically. It provides clear insights and practical tools so you can decide what to remove and what to keep — reducing the risk of damaging your system.You stay in control of what you choose to delete.

Safety note: system files (for example .sys) are identified and should not be deleted.
